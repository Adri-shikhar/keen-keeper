# 👥 KeenKeeper — Keep Your Friendships Alive

> A modern web app to track, nurture, and maintain meaningful friendships with reminders, timelines, and analytics.

## 📋 Overview

**KeenKeeper** is a friendship management app that helps you stay connected with people who matter. Track your interactions, set contact goals, and visualize your friendship patterns through intuitive timelines and analytics.

Whether you're juggling multiple circles of friends or want to be more intentional about the people in your life, KeenKeeper makes it easy to remember when you last reached out and when it's time to reconnect.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. **Friend Dashboard**
- View all your friends in a beautiful grid layout
- See contact status (Overdue, Almost Due, On Track)
- Filter by tags (Work, Family, Hobby, etc.)
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop

### 2. **Friend Details & Quick Check-In**
- Detailed friend profiles with contact history
- One-click Call, Text, or Video logging
- Automatic timeline entry creation
- Toast notifications for interactions

### 3. **Timeline Tracking**
- Visual timeline of all your interactions
- Chronological history with dates and times
- Filter by interaction type (Call, Text, Video)
- Shows exactly when you last connected

### 4. **Friendship Analytics**
- Beautiful Pie Chart showing interaction distribution
- See at a glance: Text vs Call vs Video breakdowns
- Visual representation of your communication patterns

### 5. **Responsive Mobile-First Design**
- Desktop navbar with full navigation
- Mobile hamburger menu for smaller screens
- Fully responsive on all device sizes
- Smooth animations and transitions

### 6. **Smart Status Management**
- Color-coded friend statuses
- Configurable contact goals (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Next due date tracking
- Archive and delete options (UI-ready)
